Principle Indicator
Completeness datasets should be complete, protect privacy, and include explanatory metadata
Primacy datasets should be primary source data and include details on how it was collected
Timeliness made available in a timely fashion and prioritize data that is time sensitive.
Ease of Physical and Electronic Access datasets should be accessible, easy to discover, access, and consume
Machine readability information should be stored in widely-used, machine-friendly formats, and include docs
Non-discrimination anyone can access the data without having to identify themselves or provide justification
Commonly owned or open Standards use open standards
Licensing release into the public domain
Permanence maintain information online in perpetuity

who are the customers
  • Green   Developers or people who want to do advanced modeling and/or programming with the data
  • Lime   AFF users who want to access tables and mainly volume data, and potentially build something or incorporate a layer into their own tool
  • Yellow   AFF users who want to download volume data and then run stats and/or manipulate data
  • Orange   Show me the #s on the site or let me download just the data I need but I'm not that technical
  • Blue   Small scale, informal data consumer, not familiar with Census data holdings and just wants to see raw number on the site
